Answering a research question

Maximize the quality and quantity of information you get from your data. Our team has a range of technical expertise, including:

  • Descriptive statistics, study design, and data visualization
  • Predictive modelling, data mining, machine learning
  • Pharmacoepidemiology
  • Causal inference, propensity scores, instrumental variable analysis
  • Bayesian statistics, time series forecasting
  • Spatial statistics, GIS
  • Bioinformatics, high-throughput sequencing data

Our team has expertise in the modern tools that will help you meet your goals, such as R, Stata, SAS, SPSS, SQL, Matlab, python, Latex, markdown, cloud computing, and more.

We also encourage all our clients to engage in reproducible research. Using tools such as version control and Markdown, we can teach you how to track, share and reproduce your work, either internally among your own team or with the larger scientific community online.



Designing a study

The best time to consult a statistician is before you collect or select your data. Our team can help you:

  • Conceive of and design a study or research methodology
  • Perform sample size or power calculations
  • Develop a research protocol



Individual or group training

Whether you are a student or professional, we can create an individualized learning experience designed to help to achieve your goals. We provide one-on-one services (or small group workshops) helping clients with: data cleaning and management, statistical modelling, interpretation and manuscript/report preparation.



Getting published

Our team has years of experience on both sides of the peer-review process. We can help you identify a target journal, ensure the methods section of your paper is high-quality, and prepare publication-ready graphics to better communicate your findings.
